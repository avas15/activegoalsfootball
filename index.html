Imran Ali

Founder & Head Coach — Active Goals Football

Welcome to Active Goals Football! I'm Imran, a passionate and dedicated football coach on a mission to get kids off screens, out on the pitch, and falling in love with the beautiful game. Every child is welcome here — no trials, no pressure, just football, fun and confidence building.

I believe football has the power to unite communities and bring people together. Whether your child is a complete beginner or an aspiring player, I tailor every session to build skills, confidence, and a genuine love for the sport.

My coaching philosophy is simple: make it fun, make it active, and watch players grow.
