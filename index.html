Imran Ali

Founder & Head Coach — Active Goals Football

I'm Imran, founder and head coach of Active Goals Football. I run structured, development-focused coaching sessions for young players aged 8–15 in Luton — helping them improve their technique, build confidence, and get active off their screens.

Every session is planned with clear progression in mind. Players develop fundamentals — passing, control, shooting, movement, and game understanding — in a safe, positive and encouraging environment where every child is supported to grow at their own pace.

My focus is simple: good coaching, steady development, and a positive experience for every young player who walks onto the pitch.
