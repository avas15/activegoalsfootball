About Your Coach

Coach Photo

Imran Ali

Founder & Head Coach

Welcome to Active Goals Football! I'm Imran, a passionate and dedicated football coach on a mission to get kids off screens, out on the pitch, and falling in love with the beautiful game.

I hold the FA Introduction to Coaching Football certificate from FA Education, am fully DBS checked, and First Aid certified, ensuring a safe and professional environment for every session.

I believe football has the power to unite communities and bring people together. Whether you're a beginner taking your first touch or an aspiring player looking to sharpen your technique, I tailor every session to build skills, confidence, and a genuine love for the sport. My coaching philosophy is simple: make it fun, make it active, and watch players grow.

What We Offer

Coaching sessions designed to suit every player

1-to-1 Coaching

Personalised individual training sessions focused entirely on your development. Perfect for players wanting to fast-track their improvement with tailored drills and expert feedback.

  • Bespoke training plan
  • Technical skill development
  • Position-specific coaching
  • Detailed progress feedback

Small Group Sessions

Train with 2-4 players in a focused small group environment. Great for friends who want to improve together while still getting plenty of individual attention.

  • 2-4 players per group
  • Competitive skill challenges
  • Team play development
  • Social and fun atmosphere

Group Training

Sessions for 5-8 players focusing on team skills, match play, and game awareness. An affordable way to train regularly and develop all-round footballing ability.

  • 5-8 players per session
  • Match-based scenarios
  • Tactical awareness
  • Great value coaching

Holiday Camps

Action-packed football camps during school holidays. Half-day and full-day options available to keep kids active, engaged, and improving their game.

  • Half-day & full-day options
  • Skills, games & tournaments
  • Suitable for all abilities
  • Fun, safe environment

Birthday Parties

Make their special day unforgettable with a football-themed birthday party! Professional coaching, fun games, and mini tournaments for up to 15 children.

  • Up to 15 children
  • 2 hours of football fun
  • Games & mini tournaments
  • Medals for all players
