💡 How to update the timetable

  1. Edit the sessions below — add, change, or remove as needed
  2. Click the big "Generate Schedule Code" button
  3. Click "Copy to Clipboard"
  4. Open schedule.js on your computer and replace everything with what you copied
  5. Save the file — your website will update!
